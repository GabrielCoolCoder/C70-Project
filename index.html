Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The church is one of the popular places in the school where the students spend their free time to pray. Mass is held every friday for the catholic students.

School Library

Was built in 1980

The library houses a huge collection of books both subject books and others. The students are allowed to borrow up to 5 books free of cost.

School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The auditorium can house more than 500 people. All the cultural and academic events are held here.
